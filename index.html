Our Story

Welcome to Legends Rooftop Pub & Restaurant, where the vibrant spirit of Kasi culture meets the sophistication of rooftop dining. Located in the heart of KwaJ, Umlazi, we've been serving the community with authentic township flavors since our establishment.

Our passion for great food, cold drinks, and creating memorable experiences has made us a beloved gathering spot for locals and visitors alike. From our signature braai dishes to our famous kota sandwiches, every meal is prepared with love and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Join us on our rooftop terrace and experience why we're called Legends - where every meal is a celebration of Kasi life!
